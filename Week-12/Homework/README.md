# Week 12 – Homework

## Assignment 12: Final Project — A Networked Interactive System of Your Own Design

**Duration:** 4 Weeks (Week 12–16) · **Tools:** Student's Choice — must include ≥2 network protocols from the course

### Overview

Propose, build, and present an original networked interactive system that reflects your emerging practice. The project must demonstrate mastery of at least two protocols covered in the course, involve live or real-time data, and be situated within a clear conceptual or critical framework. You will present in a public critique in the final week.

---

### Undergraduate Requirements

- Submit a 1-page proposal by Week 12 with a concept statement, technical plan, and protocol list.
- The system must integrate at least 2 protocols (e.g., WebSocket + MQTT, OSC + Web MIDI).
- Deliver a working prototype for mid-review in Week 14; incorporate feedback for final.
- Final deliverables: live demo, GitHub repo, 1-page artist statement, technical diagram.
- Participate in group critique: present 8 minutes, respond to feedback 5 minutes.

### Graduate Extensions

- Submit a 2-page proposal with concept, related works (min. 3 cited), technical architecture, and risk assessment.
- The system must integrate at least 3 protocols and involve multi-device or multi-user interaction.
- Build a full technical specification document (min. 4 pages) covering schemas, event flows, and deployment.
- Final deliverables: live installation or performance, full GitHub repo, 500-word artist statement, technical spec.
- Lead a 15-minute public presentation followed by a 10-minute Q&A; submit a revised final document 1 week later.

### Critique Criteria

Ambition, technical depth, conceptual coherence, presentation, documentation
